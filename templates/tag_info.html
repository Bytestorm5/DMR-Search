Tag Guide

Tags should generally be short descriptors (around 1-2 words) of whatever is being uploaded. These can be whatever you want for the most part, however there are standard for the more common ones:

Seasons are always represented as a "s" followed by the season number, and should always be included in the tags. (ex. s19, s17, s5)

Weapons should always be tagged with its type (lfr, sidearm, auto, pulse, gl, etc.), its energy type (kinetic, solar, arc, void, etc.), and rarity (rare, legendary, exotic, etc.).

Armor should always be tagged with its type (arms, chest, head, legs, class item), its energy type (kinetic, solar, arc, void, etc.), and rarity (rare, legendary, exotic, etc.).

NPCs should always be labeled with species (fallen, cabal, hive, etc.)

All items should also be tagged with their broader type (weapon, armor, NPC, map, shader, sparrow, etc.).

Beyond that you can add basically whatever you want- more (relevant) tags are generally better because it makes it easier to search for a given item. The reason there's a standard at all for tags is to establish a language that people can use in their search to accurately find things. Otherwise, a search like "s19" might pull things tagged as "s19" but not "Season 19" or "Season of the Seraph," which can lead to confusion. Having a standard means that you can type "s19 void pulse" without having to try changing the terms around to get the right result.
